Our Mission

The FitnessGram Pacer test is a multi-stage capacity test, that progressively gets more difficult as it continues. The running speed starts slowly, but gets faster when you hear this sound: *bloop de bloop*. A single lap will be completed when you hear this sound: *beep*. Remember to run in a straight line, and run as long as possible. The second time you fail to complete a lap before the sound, your test is over. The test will begin on the word START. On your mark, get ready, START!
